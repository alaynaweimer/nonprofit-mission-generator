## ‚öñÔ∏è When to Take Legal Action

### **Consider legal action if:**
- Repeated violations after cease and desist
- Large company with deep pockets
- Significant revenue loss to you
- Blatant, willful infringement
- Damage to your brand/reputation

### **Don't bother with legal action if:**
- Small-time violator
- Good faith mistake
- Quick to comply
- Not worth the cost
- Better to just license them

### **Legal Action Costs:**
- Lawyer consultation: $300-500
- Cease and desist letter: $500-1,000
- Copyright registration: $35-$65
- Actual lawsuit: $10,000-$100,000+

**Remember:** The goal is revenue, not revenge!
